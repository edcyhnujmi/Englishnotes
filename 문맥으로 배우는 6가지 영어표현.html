얘기 들었어?
Did you hear?(Have you heard?)

킴 해고됐대.
Kim was(got) fired.

[과거진행형] ~하고 있었다. : was ~ing
actually + I was just ~ing...
어. 그렇지 않아도 지금 박이랑 그 얘기하고 있었어.
Yeah, actually, I was just talking to Park about that.

장난아니다. 그렇지?
Crazy~,right?

글쎄, 너네는 어떤지 모르겠는데 난 솔직히 말해서 별로 안 놀랍던데?
Well, I don't know about you guys, but to be honest, I wasn't so(very) surprised.

왜? 그게 무슨 말이야?/소리야?
Why? What do you mean?/What are you talking about?
